PADI Scuba Diver Course

The PADI Scuba Diver is the first level of certification. It is perfect for someone that loves the water, but doesnâ€™t have the time to complete the PADI Open Water Course.

There are 5 chapters in the PADI Open Water Manual but for the PADI Scuba Diver course you need to complete and review only Chapters 1 - 3. There is also a quick quiz for each chapter. There is no final exam. You must also complete the first 3 out of the 5 Confined Water Training sessions, where you learn the skills that you will use whenever you dive. Finally you will complete the first 2 Open Water training dives where you will have the chance to practice the skills you mastered in Confined Water.

As a certified PADI Scuba Diver you are now certified to a depth of 12m as long as you dive with a PADI Professional.

The PADI Scuba Diver certification is part of the PADI Open Water Course and you can take the upgrade course any time.If you are already a PADI Scuba Diver, ask us about upgrading to PADI Open Water Diver!
