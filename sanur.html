Description

Sanur channel and dive sites along Sanur reef has an abundance of life and, since it is our house reef, we can easily go for 1 or 2 dives depending on how energetic you are feeling that day! You will be provided with everything needed for the dives: transportation to the dive sites in our very own Balinese local boat, called a jukung, top quality customer service from our dedicated PADI dive instructor, fruit and water after the dives, all necessary dive equipment, towel, all taxes and fees and dive insurance.
