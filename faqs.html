Save time! Here's some Frequently Asked Questions to help you find the answer you're looking for.

Where can I dive?
On the ocean..
Amed
  • The surf and courses are scheduled as followed:
      • 08.00 am – 10.00 am
      10.00 am – 12.00
      01.00 pm – 03.00
  • Courses levels are dependent on the tides.
  • Beginner courses are held on mid tides adding safety and ease to the learning process.
  • Advance courses are held on the high tides offering more comprehensive conditions and powerful waves.
  • Reef trips are generally held in the morning departing from the school at 7.30am to be in the water by 8am in Canggu or 8.30am in the boat in Sanur returning to the school by 11 am.
  • Adult private will be scheduled dependent on the level of the surfer and the according required conditions.
  • Kids’ courses are generally held on the low tide, due to the shallow water and small waves.
Sanur
  • The courses in Sanur are scheduled around the tide (available water depth) in the Sanur Lagoon.
  • Courses will be scheduled from 8am – 5pm.
  • We require a minimum depth of 1.0m of water to operate our boats for surfing boat trip and taxi out.
  • Courses levels are dependent on the tides
  • Beginner Surfing courses are held on mid to high tide offering safer condition and deeper water.
  • Advance Surfing courses can be held on all tides preferably on mid tide since the waves are more powerful.
  • Stand Up Paddle level 1 and guided paddle require only a 0.8m depth.
  • Beginner Kite Surfing courses can be held on low tides as they don’t require water, just light wind.
  • Kite Surfing level 2 & 3 require a wind around 10knots to be held on all tides using the deep lagoon to the side of the school.
  • Kite Surfing courses are held all year however June – September are the best month for consistent wind.
  • All Wakeboarding levels require 10m depths to be held or using the deep lagoon to the side of the school.
  • All Windsurfing levels require 10m depths, from light to strong wind.
